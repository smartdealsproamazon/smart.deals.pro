Gaming Setup Tips

Choose the Right Monitor

Look for high refresh rates (144Hz+) and low input lag for competitive gaming. 1ms response time is ideal for fast-paced games.

Audio Matters

Good gaming headsets provide positional audio advantage. Look for surround sound support and clear microphone quality.

Precision Peripherals

Gaming mice with adjustable DPI and mechanical keyboards with tactile feedback can improve your performance significantly.

Stable Connection

For online gaming, ensure stable internet connection. Wired connections are preferred over Wi-Fi for competitive play.
