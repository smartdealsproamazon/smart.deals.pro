How do I get started with SmartDeals Pro?

Getting started is easy:

  • Create a free account on our platform
  • Complete your profile verification
  • Browse our product catalog or list your own products
  • Start promoting products and earning commissions
  • Track your performance through our analytics dashboard

Is there a cost to join SmartDeals Pro?

No, joining SmartDeals Pro is completely free. We believe in making affiliate marketing accessible to everyone. You can sign up, list products, and start earning commissions without any upfront costs or monthly fees.

SmartDeals Pro operates globally and supports affiliates and customers from most countries worldwide. We have special programs for major markets including the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and European Union countries. Check our terms of service for specific country availability.

Products & Listings

How do I list my products on SmartDeals Pro?

Listing products is straightforward:

  • Log into your affiliate dashboard
  • Click "Add New Product" or "Product Submission"
  • Fill in product details, pricing, and descriptions
  • Upload high-quality images
  • Set your commission rate
  • Submit for review (typically approved within 24-48 hours)

What types of products can I sell?

We accept a wide range of products:
