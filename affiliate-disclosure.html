What Are Affiliate Links?

An affiliate link is a special URL that contains an affiliate's ID or username. When you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, the affiliate (in this case, SmartDeals Pro) earns a commission from the sale. This commission comes from the retailer or merchant, not from you as the customer.

Our Affiliate Partnerships

SmartDeals Pro participates in affiliate marketing programs with various retailers and merchants, including but not limited to:

  • Amazon Associates Program
  • Commission Junction (CJ Affiliate)
  • ShareASale
  • Impact Radius
  • ClickBank
  • Various individual brand affiliate programs

This means that when you click on certain links on our website and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

How We Use Affiliate Links

We use affiliate links in several ways on our website:

  • Product Reviews: When we review products, we may include affiliate links to where you can purchase those products.
  • Comparison Pages: Our product comparison pages may contain affiliate links to help you find the best deals.
  • Deal Pages: Our deals and promotions pages feature affiliate links to special offers and discounts.
  • Content Articles: Some of our informational articles may include affiliate links to relevant products or services.

No Additional Cost to You

Using our affiliate links does not cost you anything extra. The price you pay for products or services remains the same whether you use our affiliate links or go directly to the retailer's website. The commission we earn comes from the retailer's marketing budget, not from your purchase price.

Our Commitment to Honest Reviews
